Brian S. Fugate

Associate Dean for Graduate Programs and Research. Chair, Supply Chain Management. Oren Harris Endowed Chair in Transportation.

Bio

Brian S. Fugate serves as Associate Dean for Graduate Programs and Research at the Sam M. Walton College of Business, where he leads college efforts that lift graduate education and research impact. He also chairs the J.B. Hunt Department of Supply Chain Management and holds the Oren Harris Endowed Chair in Transportation. A former Fulbright Senior Research Scholar and past Co-Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Supply Chain Management, he studies how firms integrate demand and supply, move knowledge, and build sustainable, technology-enabled supply networks. His work appears in the Journal of Supply Chain Management, Journal of Operations Management, Decision Sciences, Academy of Management Journal, and other outlets. He co-authored *The Blockchain Toolkit* and *Operations and Supply Chain Management: Enhancing Competitiveness and Customer Value*. Before academia he worked in logistics, supplier development, and industrial engineering in the airline, consumer packaged goods, and automotive sectors.
