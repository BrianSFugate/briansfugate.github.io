Bio

Dr. Brian S. Fugate serves as Associate Dean of Graduate Programs and Research in the Sam M. Walton College of Business at the University of Arkansas, where he leads college-wide initiatives that elevate graduate education, strengthen research infrastructure, and amplify faculty impact. He also chairs the J.B. Hunt Department of Supply Chain Management and holds the Oren Harris Endowed Chair in Transportation. Dr. Fugate previously served as a Fulbright Senior Research Scholar at the MIT-Zaragoza Logistics Center in Spain and as Co-Editor-in-Chief of the Journal of Supply Chain Management.

His research examines the strategic integration of demand and supply, knowledge flows, and sustainability across global supply networks, with emphasis on inter-organizational collaboration and enabling technologies. His work appears in Journal of Supply Chain Management, Journal of Operations Management, Decision Sciences, Academy of Management Journal, and related outlets. He co-authored The Blockchain Toolkit: A Supply Chain Manager’s Guide to Understanding and Implementing Blockchain and Operations and Supply Chain Management: Enhancing Competitiveness and Customer Value.

An award-winning educator, Dr. Fugate has received AACSB’s “Innovations That Inspire” recognition and multiple nominations for the Panhellenic Council’s University Professor of the Year. He teaches undergraduate, graduate, executive, and professional development courses in on-campus and online formats and advances active learning through simulations, live industry projects, and software-supported pedagogy.
